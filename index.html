Corbeau House was originally a large Victorian Villa and has been sympathetically renovated to create 5 stylish separate apartments. Original features help create a sense of space and charm, including high ceilings, sash bay windows and brick exposed walls. Externally the property has also been equally well renovated blending both local materials with original mosaic tiled entrance hall, large doorway and Purbeck Stone front wall.
Kittiwake is a one bed ground floor apartment and has been finished equally to a high standard with luxurious carpeting throughout, traditional cast iron style radiators, high quality walnut finish doors, and modern standard insulation and sound-proofing.
The open plan living/kitchen area has a beautiful original brick fireplace with electric log effect fire, the large bay window offers seasonal sea glimpses and views to Ballard Down.
